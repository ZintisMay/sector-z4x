What is Sector Z4X?

A space opera board game for 2-4 players

You've finally done it, your scientists have discovered faster than light travel. As you point your starships at the stars, soon into your journey you discover that you are not alone. First contact is soon followed by second and third, and it becomes clear that there are many civilizations in the galaxy. You soon learn that while small, these civilizations have come together to form the Senate, an collaboration between all the sentient races of the galaxy.
