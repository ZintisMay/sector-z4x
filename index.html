Sector Z4X
Epic 4x Space Empires Board Game

2-4 players

40 Minutes per player

You've finally done it, your scientists have discovered faster than light travel! As you aim your starships at the sky, you learn you are not alone.

First contact is followed by second and third. You find many civilizations in the galaxy. Mostly small, together they form an organiztion called the Senate. The Senate has a strong influence over the galaxy, pooling their resources to maintain order, and ensure the survival of the many (or maybe the few).

You meet with the leaders of your empire and decide that to ensure your species' best chance of survival, you will integrate yourselves into the galactic community, take a leading role in the Senate, and lead the galaxy into the next era!

Description

Sector Z4X is designed to provide a complete, fulfilling 4X gaming experience. A mainstay of digital games, 4X stands of Explore, Expand, Exploit, and Exterminate, and are the pillars of the empire building genre.

Z4X uses Euro mechanics to streamline the gameplay while still being epic in scope, with a emergent story arc. Turns are fast and most of the mechanics are easy to understand. There is a ton of distinct content in the map, technologies, factions, and politics which come together to provide a unique experience every time you play. This provides each game with a sense of exploring the unknown as a different combination of tiles and cards come together.
