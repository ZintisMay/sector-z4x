What is Sector Z4X?

A space opera board game for 2-4 players

40 Minutes per player

You've finally done it, your scientists have discovered faster than light travel. As you aim your starships at the sky, you soon learn you are not alone.

First contact is followed by second and third, and it becomes clear that there are many civilizations in the galaxy. Mostly small, together they form the Senate, a collaboration between all the sentient races of the galaxy. The Senate has a strong influence over the galaxy, as all the races come together for a common purpose for the good of the many (or maybe just the few).

After much deliberation, you decide that your species' best chance of survival is to integrate yourselves into the galactic community, take a leading role in the Senate, and lead the galaxy into the next era.
